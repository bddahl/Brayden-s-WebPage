About Me

Life Goals and Vision

Goals:
When thinking about life goals, I tend to think about what's most desirable in life: Family, God, Health, Happiness. I have personal plans to reach all of my goals in each of these areas, and I know with faith and consistent effort, that I can reach my goals of having a loving family, having God centered in my life, keeping my body healthy, and having happiness in me to be able to spread it to others.

Vision:
In 1 year I see myself thriving in the accounting program at BYU, performing my best at track meets, staying close to God, and enjoying life with Kali. In 5 years I can envision myself as a father of a young family living in a house in Arizona while working and thriving at Deloitte. In 10 years I want to be reaching my full potential in every aspect of my life and giving everything I can to God and my family. I will continue to learn and grow as my wife and I love and care for our children.

Student

Athlete

I am an NCAA D1 athlete on the BYU Men's Track & Field team. I am blessed to have the opportunity to race against the fastest athletes in the US. My primary event is the 400 meter hurdles, but have run various others such as the 60m, 200m, 500m, and 4x400m relay. I love the competitive nature of sports and being able to test my limits each day while I learn and grow.

LDS Mission

For 2 years (2021-2023) I served as a volunteer missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of latter-day Saints in Fort Lauderdale, Florida and in Lisbon, Portugal. The positive experiences I had with God and with countless numbers of His children during this time cannot be described in fulness in this limited space. From serving others, helping people move, talking and teaching of Jesus Christ, to baptizing people in His name, being a servant of the Lord was nothing less than celestial.

Family

Work

As my love for education and family continues to grow, it becomes necessary to provide for oneself and his family. While there I learned much about the work culture and how to become a leader at such a big company. I am grateful for the opportunites they have given me and am excited to work with them in the future.
